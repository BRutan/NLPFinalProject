Certification of the Chief Financial Officer
Pursuant to 18 U.S.C. Section 1350
In connection with the CarMax, Inc. (the "company") Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended February 28, 2019 , as filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on the date hereof (the "Report"), I, Thomas W. Section 1350, as adopted pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002, that, to my knowledge:
